🧆Tabriz meatballs

Tabriz meatballs (Persian: کوفته تبریزی, romanized: Kufteh Tabrizi), also known as Koofteh Tabrizi, is an Iranian meatball recipe from the city of Tabriz.The dish normally includes a big meat ball with meat, rice, yellow split peas, herbs and other ingredients and its juice which is served in a separate dish with shredded Sangak or Lavash bread before the main course.
